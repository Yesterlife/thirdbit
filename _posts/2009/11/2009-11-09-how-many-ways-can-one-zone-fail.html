--- title: "How Many Ways Can OneZone Fail?" date: 2009-11-09 08:42:46 --- Stop me if you've heard this one... I'm at the court house waiting to find out if I'll be selected for jury duty. The only WiFi network available is OneZone, but:
  1. The signup pages are all HTTP, not HTTPS (which means my credit card information and home address just flew threw the ether unencrypted), and
  2. Once I sign up (yeah, I did it anyway, I'm that desperate for bandwidth) the system echoes back my user ID and password on another unsecure page in plaintext.
Who are these people? And why are they still employed? *sigh* Later: it may be insecure, but at least it's slow...
