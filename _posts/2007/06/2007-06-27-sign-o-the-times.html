--- title: "Sign o' the Times" date: 2007-06-27 11:40:28 --- John Lam reports that Steve Yegge has ported Ruby on Rails to Javascript. Yegge himself seems a little nonplussed by all the fuss, while Joe Gregorio thinks it signifies little. I disagree: I've said for two years that Javascript has a real shot at being the hottest language of 2010, and efforts like this (however misguidedly heroic) are pointers on that road.
