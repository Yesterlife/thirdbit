--- title: "CAST'08 in Toronto" date: 2007-12-16 07:49:29 --- Via Adam Goucher: the Third Annual Conference of the Association for Software Testing (CAST) will be held in Toronto on July 14-16, 2008. The keynote speaker is Gerry Weinberg; the theme is "Beyond the Boundaries: Interdisciplinary Approaches to Software Testing". Coming as it does on the heels of the announcement that Agile'08 will be here next August, it's shaping up to be quite a summer.
