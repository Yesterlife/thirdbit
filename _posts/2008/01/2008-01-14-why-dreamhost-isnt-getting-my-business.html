--- title: "Why Dreamhost Isn't Getting My Business" date: 2008-01-14 12:40:20 --- I decided late last year that the time had come to host this site commercially (it currently lives on a little old machine sitting in some donated rack space in downtown Toronto). My first thought was Dreamhost, but when I tried to sign up this morning, they insisted that I stuff my credit card details into my Google account for processing. One less customer for Dreamhost...
