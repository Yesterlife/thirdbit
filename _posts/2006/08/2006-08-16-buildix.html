--- title: "Buildix" date: 2006-08-16 09:03:09 --- Buildix (from ThoughtWorks) shows just how commoditized web-based project management tools have become. It's basically Linux-on-a-CD with Subversion, CruiseControl, Trac, and user management ready for use. No sign of tagging, mailing lists, or a scripting interface, though, which are three features we considered must-haves for DrProject. I wonder how hard it would be to package DrP this way?
(And for regular readers, I'm pleased to announce that Greg Lapouchnian has acquired the drproject.org domain to host long-term development. Thanks, GregL.
