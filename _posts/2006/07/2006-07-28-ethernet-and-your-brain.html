--- title: "Ethernet and Your Brain" date: 2006-07-28 10:14:17 --- "Each of your eyes transfers information to your brain at about the same speed as a fast Ethernet connection." (as reported in this New Scientist article). That's about 8.75 megabits/sec (extrapolating from guinea pig data). Our brains could handle more --- kind of funny to discover that even evolution leads to bandwidth-limited systems.
