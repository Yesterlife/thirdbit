--- title: "Agile Commenting" date: 2005-04-03 17:22:23 --- The new hype in software development is Agile Programming Languages. These languages try to speed development time by ignoring traditional principles such as static typing. Unfortunately despite these advances, certain instructors still insist on the tradition of commenting code. Enter Agile Commenting in the form of the Commentator. What better way to make that last minute code cutoff?
